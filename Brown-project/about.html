About Pat's Pastries

Pat was born and raised in North Carolina and has had an immense love for baking ever since she was young. Before she even graduated high school, she had won numerous local baking competitions and had begun selling her baked goods. She went on to study culinary arts in college and studied abroad in Paris, learning how to make a variety of cuisines.

Pat begun Pat's Pastries out of her kitchen in 2000, moved to a brick and mortar store in 2005, and the business has now been satisfying customers for 24 years in total!

Our bakery is located at 1234 Bakery Lane, Greensboro, North Carolina 27411!
